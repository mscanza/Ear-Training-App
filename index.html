Instructions

Ear Training is the ability to understand how one pitch or note relates to another, and how multiple pitches combine to form chords. With some practice, you will be able to recognize how these notes and chords relate to eachother in all music you listen to!

High Note

One basic way to do this is to compare 2 pitches, and discern which pitch has a higher frequency than the other, or if they are in fact the same pitch. (Also called 'unison')

Listen to both pitches and determine which is higher. Make sure your sound is on and have fun!
Adjust audio to low. Listen to both pitches. Which pitch is higher?
